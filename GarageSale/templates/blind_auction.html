Charity Auction - David Lloyd Ipswich Trial memberships

Following a very generous sponsorship package from David Lloyd Club in Ipswich we are able to offer the following trial memberships for Auction:
  • One Individual 14 day trial pass at the Ipswich Club - retail value approximately £50
  • Two family (2 adult & 2 children) 14 day trial passes packs for the Ipswich Club - each pack includes 4 50% food vouchers - retail value approximately £150 per pack of passes and vouchers.
We are going to auction these prizes off in a Blind Auction that opens on 1st June and closes at midnight on 23rd June, the day of the Garage Sale.

Please note that the vouchers are marked by David Lloyd as valid until the 31st July 2024 only. The Garage Sale Foundation have no ability to change these conditions..

If you want to get your hands on one of these 3 prizes simply:
  • Email Auction.BranthamGarageSale@gmail.com
  • Include your monetary bid amount in pounds and
  • either "Individual" or "Family" in the Email subject
Or just follow these handy links where you only need to add your bid amount :
  • "Individual"
  • "Family"
On the 24th June we will check the emails and the highest bid for the Individual pack and the two highest bids for the family packs will win the appropriate items.

The successful bidders will be notified by Email on the 24th June and will have until 28th June to submit their payment. If payment is not made by this deadline the prize will go to the next highest bidder.

Full rules and conditions are listed below.

Terms and Conditions

  1. Only emails sent to Auction.BranthamGarageSale@gmail.com will be counted.
  2. Emails will be discarded if they are incorrect, this includes :
    • Emails received before 1st June 2024, or after June 23rd 2024
    • Emails that do not include either the text 'Individual' or 'Family'
    • Emails that do not include a bid amount.
  3. If Multiple bids from the same email address are received, then the more recent received bid is accepted.
  4. The bids will be examined on June 24th and the winners selected as follows :
    • The highest bid for the Individual voucher will win the item
    • The two highest bids for the family voucher packs will win one pack per bid.
    In the case of a tie between bids, the earliest bid will be considered the winner.
  5. The Winning bidders will be notified by 24th June 2024, and bids must be paid by BACS transfer by 28th June 2024.
  6. If the winning bidder does not submit payment by the deadline, the next highest bid for the items will be notified.
  7. Vouchers can be collected once Payment is received from 19, Merriam Close, Brantham - please bring some form of ID.
  8. Winning bids are Charity donations, and we regret that they cannot be refunded once paid.
  9. David lLoyd may apply their own terms and conditions which are outside the control of the Charity.
  10. The vouchers are only valid for the Ipswich Club
  11. In the event of no winning bid for any of the items - the Trustee committee will make a further decision on how best to make use of the un-won items for the benefit of the Charity.
