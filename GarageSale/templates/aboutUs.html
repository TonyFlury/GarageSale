Brantham Garage Sale - About Us

For many years the Brantham Garage Sale has been a feature of the summer Schedule in Brantham, Suffolk. The community comes together across the village on one day to host a range of sales on their driveways and front-gardens, with sellers donating to the Garage Sale fund and some sellers also donating to good causes close to them.

The Charity sale started in 2003, in tribute to Carla Dawson (a local teenager) and the care she and her family received at St Elizabeth's Hospice in Ipswich, and the Hospice continues to be a significant recipient of the proceeds of the Garage Sale Event.

The Sale event in 2023 was the 20th Anniversary event, and was potentially going to be the last one, due to the current organiser stepping down, and with no clear replacement.

The Brantham Garage Sale Foundation

In late 2023 the Brantham Garage Sale Foundation was founded as a small charity dedicated to ensuring that the Sale Event continued into the future.

The Charity is run by a small committee of trustees (currently 5 members). We abide by the Charities Commission rules on how small charities should be run (this includes a double signature bank account for the charity funds). We are not currently a registered charity (we don't have a charity number), although that is something the trustees keep under regular review.

One benefit from being a charity with a formal set of governing rules, is that we are able to apply to the HMRC for gift-aid, further boosting the money we raise for those good causes.

It is the key principle of the charity that 100% of all the proceeds donated by the sellers will be dispersed to the designated good causes. Any costs that the charity incur (for instance advertising and other items) will be covered by our generous sponsors, and any surplus sponsorship funds if possible will also be dispersed to the same causes.

The Garage Sale 2024

The Garage Sale in 2024 is set for Sunday, June 23rd, and we are keeping our fingers and toes crossed for reasonable weather for the day.
The 2024 Sale will be raising funds for the St Elizabeth's Hospice, and the 1st Brantham Scout and Guide Group.
