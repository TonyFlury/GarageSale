Donating before or after the sale

We have a number of ways you can donate to us, either before or after your sale. Any donation is welcome no matter how small: Every single penny will help the charities that we are supporting,

Direct to our Bank Account via BACS

If you have access to online banking then you can make a direct donation via BACS - use the following details :

  • Account Type : Business
  • Name : Brantham Garage Sale Foundation
  • Acc # : 26992063
  • Sort Code : 30 99 50

Paying by cash

You can pay by cash in a number of places in Brantham as listed below. In all cases please ensure the cah is within a sealed envelope with your name and address and postcode clearly written on the envelope.

You can drop off your cash donations at :

  • Chloe's Cafe, Acacia Court - until the 29th June only
  • 19, Merriam Close, Brantham. CO11 1RY
  • 7, Western's Close, Brantham. CO11 1RS
  • 33, Brooklands Road, CO11 1RP
  • 2, Hilltop, Brantham Hill, CO11 1SR
  • 3, Sycamore Way, CO11 1TL

It is entirely safe for you to post your cash donations through the letterbox in its sealed envelope.

Donation deadline

Please ensure all donations either BACS or cash are made by Sunday 7th, July. This ensures that the Trustees are able to announce the total in the August issue of the Brantham Bugle.
