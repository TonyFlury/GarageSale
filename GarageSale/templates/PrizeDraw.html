2026 Prize Draw

We are very pleased to announce the launch of our 2026 Prize Draw. Tickets are available from Wednesday 25th March from a variety of places around the village. The tickets are only £1.00 each, and all proceeds go into our 2026 fundraising campaign. The Draw will be made on Sunday 28th June - around 3pm outside Chloes cafe in Brantham. Winners will be notified by 5th July.
