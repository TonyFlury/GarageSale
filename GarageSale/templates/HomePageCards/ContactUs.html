Nominate a good cause

Every year approximately In September we pick a good cause to fund-raise for next year. To be eligible the good cause must have some benefit to the community or residents of Brantham.

That good cause will get 50% of all of the funs we raise during that years campaign, while the other 50% will go to St Elizabeth's Hospice in Ipswich.
