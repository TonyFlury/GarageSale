Donating to the Charity

Donation Basics

We welcome cash donations at any time : we have cash collection tins in a few places across the village, between January and the Sale Event in June. You can also donate via BACS at any time.

Of the money donated by residents, every single penny is paid out to the charities. The costs of posters, leaflets, insurance and the rest of the running costs are covered through sponsorship from local businesses.

Sale Event Donations

For the main Garage Sale event we ask for a minimum donation of £8.00 for every sale pitch, whether you are on the map, or you set up on the day.

We would encourage you to donate more than £8.00 if you are able. We do ask that all donations for the sale event are made no later than two weeks after the event.

Although we will have volunteers Steward checking in with stall holders on the day, they will not be able to take your cash donations.
